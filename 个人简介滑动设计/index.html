More of my credentials.

I am the creator of The Femme Press - an online source for topical news, produced by and about women. If you are as passionate as I am about accurate journalism and equality, I want to collaborate with you, today!

Born and raised a southern belle in a small town called Ninety Six, my mother tells me that I was a creative and resourceful kid from the very beginning. I attended the all womens college in Columbia, SC and received my BA in Speech Therapy in 2011. After working four years as a licensed SLP assistant, I packed up and followed my partner to the frozen tundra. In my new home of Wisconsin, I became a licensed early childhood educator and worked with ages birth to five years old for the next three years. In that time, I realized that my passion for developement, education, and leadership was not meant to be limited to a specific age population. During this realization, I became more active in the Dane County community and returned to school at Madison College to obtain my degree in Web Software Development.

Currently, I am searching for ways to pave my own path. Ideally, my goals are to program and produce high quality software with a heavy focus on user accessibility. Additionally, I seek employment that actively strives for a higher quality of life, for all.
