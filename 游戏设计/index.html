John Romero

“You might not think that programmers are artists, but programming is an extremely creative profession. It's logic-based creativity.”

John Romero

Alfonso John Romero (born October 28, 1967) is best known for being the co-founder of ID Software. He has worked on titles including Wolfenstein 3D, Dangerous Dave, Hexen, Doom and Quake. His game designs, development tools, and new programming techniques created with id Software's lead programmer John D. Carmack popularized the FPS genre. Romero is known for coining the term "deathmatch".

Wolfenstein 3D - May 5, 1992

Doom - December 10, 1993
