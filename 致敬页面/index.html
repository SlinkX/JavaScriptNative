Elon Musk

Entrepreneur, Engineer, Inventor

Born in 1971 in South Africa for a Canadian Mother and South African father. He developed an interest in computers and he taught himself programming and develop a game called Blaster. At age 17, in 1989, he moved to Canada to attend Queen’s University but he left in 1992 to study business and physics at the University of Pennsylvania. He graduated with an undergraduate degree in economics and stayed for a second bachelor’s degree in physics.
