Elon Musk

Timeline

  • Born June 28, 1971 in Pretoria, Transvaal, South Africa.
  • 1981 he discovered computing using a Commodore VIC-20.
  • 1983 he taught himself computer programming and sold the code for a BASIC video game for US$500.
  • 1989 moved to Canada after obtaining Canadian citizenship through his Canadian born mother.
  • 1990 was accepted into Queens University in Kingston, Ontario for undergraduate study.
  • 1992 he transferred to the University of Pensylvania.
  • 1994 recieved a Bachelor of Science in physics from College of Arts and Sciences.
  • 1995 recieved a Bachelor of Science in economics Wharton School, and moved to California to begin a PhD in applied physics from Stanford University. However, he left the program after two days to pursue his entrepeneurial aspirations the areas of the Internet, renewable energy and outer space.
  • 1995 Elon and his brother Kimbal started Zip2, a web software company. They developed an Internet "city guide".
  • 1999 Compaq accquired Zip2 for US$307 million in cash and US$34 million in stock options.
  • 1999 Elon co-founded X.com, an online financial services and email payment company with US$10 million from the sale of Zip2.
  • 2000 Zip2 merged with anoter company called Cofinity, which had a money transfer service called PayPal. The merged company focused on PayPal and thus, the company was renamed to Paypal in 2001.
  • 2002 Paypal was accquired by eBay for US$1.5 billion in stock, of which US$165 million was given to Elon.
  • 2002 He founded Space Exploration Technologies, also known as SpaceX.
  • 2002 He became a US citizen.
  • 2003 Tesla Motors was incorporated.
  • 2006 Tesla Roadster was revelead in a small event at Barker Hangar at Santa Monica Airport
  • 2006 Musk provided the initial concept, and financial capitals for SolarCity, which was then co-founded by his cousins.
  • 2008 The first Tesla Roadster was delivered.
  • 2009 The Tesla Model S prototype vehicle was displayed at a press conference.
  • 2012 The Model S was introduced into the market in June 2012
  • 2013 He introduced and release to the public the prelimanary plans for a high-speed transportation system called the "Hyperloop".
  • 2015, Elon Musk announced the creation of OpenAI, a not-for-profit artificial intelligence (AI) research company.
